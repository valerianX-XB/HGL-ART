# V3 Client / Demo Readiness Note

## What the map now does
- Shows HGL ART v3 Manhattan market intelligence for six ZIP/ZCTA areas.
- Uses corrected positioning: HGL ART is a multilingual early childhood education / preschool-style school integrating English, Mandarin Chinese, Spanish, and art-based education.
- Supports building estimates, street opportunity, block group, census block, ZIP overview, OOH inventory, and combined opportunity views.
- Includes Under 5 / preschool-age signals, modeled building/street opportunity signals, family anchors, and OOH inventory.

## Manual checks before showing client
- Open the production build in browser and check hover stability, no horizontal scrollbar, and control-panel responsiveness.
- Capture screenshots for building, street, OOH, ZIP overview, and combined views.
- Confirm selected metrics/styles visually update immediately.
- Confirm HGL exact address and any live-demo claims.

## What not to claim
- Do not claim actual building residents, children, families, or household data.
- Do not claim OOH vacancy, price, impressions, or guaranteed availability.
- Do not claim licensed preschool/daycare/official kindergarten without client confirmation.
- Do not claim Mandarin/Spanish/multilingual staffing/curriculum beyond confirmed HGL facts.

## Suggested demo flow
1. Start with ZIP Overview and explain relative market opportunity.
2. Switch to Under 5 / preschool-age metric.
3. Show Street Opportunity and explain modeled local signals.
4. Show Building Estimate and emphasize aggregate/modelled nature.
5. Turn on OOH Inventory and filter by bookable/candidate statuses.
6. End with caveats and vendor-verification next steps.

## Suggested talking points
- The map is a decision-support tool, not individual targeting.
- Strongest use: compare relative opportunity and prioritize field/vendor checks.
- OOH list is an outreach tracker starter, not a confirmed media buy.

## Known limitations
- Browser visual QA still recommended before client presentation.
- Vendor-side OOH availability/rates must be confirmed.
- Exact HGL address and regulatory language require client confirmation.
