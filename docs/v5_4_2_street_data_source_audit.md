## Conclusion
`street_under5_capacity_signal.geojson` and `street_segments_enriched.geojson` are duplicate 2,580-feature real LineString street datasets. V5.4.2 creates a normalized canonical copy and loads only that file. No ZIP, block-group, grid, OOH-derived, or generated in-memory fallback street layer is used in normal Street Capacity Signal rendering.
